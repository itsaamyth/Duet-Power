Who we are

Duet Power Growth Journey

Duet Power was founded in 2007 in Ghaziabad. Duet Power ia operational in India with the mission & vision to make solar energy affordable, available, and hassle-free to customers looking for a better energy alternative. The company is engaged in complex supplies of industrial electrical equipment from some of the best manufacturers in this field on the market. Since its inception, our company has been implementing large solar energy projects, which develops the renewable energy market of India.


Duet Power provides a range of solar energy solutions such as Solar Panels , Solar Stations and Solar Products to residential, commercial and industrial customers. Our focus is particularly Small and Medium-sized Enterprises to afford solar, we have also set-up an in-house finance facility to provide extended payment terms to commercial and industrial customers.
Duet Power is also an official distributor of Innolia Energy private Limited the best manufacturers of solar module, Lithium battery and electric vehicle as well as supplies other brands already established on the market. Over the years of its existence, Duet Power has been able to achieve smoothness in relations with foreign and domestic partners and the best manufacturers, therefore we offer exclusively high-quality goods - uninterruptible power supplies, batteries and solar systems.
