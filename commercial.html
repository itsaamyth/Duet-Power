Commercial Services

Grid Tied Solar Plant

Grid-tied rooftop solar power plants are a great way to combine the advantages of solar power and grid power. for suppose let’s consider a hotel that has solar panels installed, but no batteries. It is night time. Without batteries, solar power cannot supply its electricity requirements during the night. But the Grid power can. The grid-tied solar power system hence takes all the electricity required from the grid during the night for this hotel.
so you can see how easy and useful it is to use Grid Tie Solar Plant specially in commercial sectors where every seconds of electricity is needed.


Benefits of Grid Tied Solar System :
  1. 1. More economical
  2. 2. The grid serves as your virtual battery
  3. 3. Save more money with net metering
  4. 4. Grid-tied systems tend to be the less expensive option
  5. 5. The grid is a reliable storage solution

Hybrid Solar Plant

Hybrid Solar is also a great way of using solar services without any worries as The Hybrid solar systems also generates power in the same way as a common grid-tie solar system but uses a special hybrid inverters and batteries to store energy for later use. This ability to store energy enables most hybrid systems to also operate as a backup power supply during a blackout, similar to a UPS system or you can take the hotel example.


Benefits of Hybrid Solar System :
  1. 1. Allows you to store excess solar or low cost (off-peak) electricity
  2. 2. The grid serves as your virtual battery
  3. 3. Most hybrid inverters have backup power capability
  4. 4. Reduces power consumption from the grid
  5. 5. Enables advanced energy management
